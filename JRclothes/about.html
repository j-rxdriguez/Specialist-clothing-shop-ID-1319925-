History:

The sock shop was first founded in 2017 by Jesus Rodriguez. Designing this brand he strove for the best quality socks, providing maximum warmth & comfort against the elements. So he designed socks that are both breathable yet insulating and gentle against the skin.

About our socks:

  • Made out of 78% Cotton, 17% Polyester, 4% Polyamide, 1% Elastane.
  • Can be machine washed.
  • Mid-height socks with excellent fit.
  • With strengthened heel and toe to last everyday wear and tear.
